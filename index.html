SITE UPDATE [18-01-2026]

Well at least this update didn't take as long as the previous...

After finally settling 150 miles(ish), I've been working on streamlining the computer vision analysis process. Common problems can be found irrespective of the context or target outcome for a vision task and to resolve that I have created a repo called Plum Process, so named after getting my car stuck in a bog and the only person who picked up to offer support being Miss Plum, an old colleague from Itsu days and friend of mine.

I've also been working on an extension of the COCO json standard, the rough workings of which can be found in my PlumProcess repo.

These afformentioned implementations have been worked on in conjunction with my day to day tasks as a Data Scientist at Cefas (the benefits of youth). I should also be posting the progress I made on 150k, the travel app I work on and off on, the goal of that being to use graph networks to automate the holiday planning process, flowcharts and rough algorithms were developed a while ago but it becomes a bit of a mountain to develop by yourself whilst working on complex end to end computer vision models. I do aim to publish more here, but it'll most likely be in the form of my handwritten notes from my remarkable. As always, feel free to shoot me a message
